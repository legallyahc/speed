Rules of the Montreal Metro Speedrun
================
AHC
2023-03-04

## General Rules

1.  Time begins when the doors close on the train at the beginning
    station.
2.  Time begins when the doors open on the train at the last un-visited
    station.
3.  Each station must be visited at the platform.
  -   Staying on the train counts!
4.  Any mode of transport aside from an automobile can be used between
    stations.
  -   e.g. a bus between two stations
5.  If possible, record a timelapse or take a picture of each station
    sign for proof.
6.  Post it somewhere (optional)!
7.  Celebrate! :)

## Co-op Rules (overwrites general rules)

1.  A team must all begin together.
2.  A team must all end together.
-   People are allowed to leave the speedrun part way through, if they
    desire
3.  A team cannot divide into more groups than allowed.
4.  Each station a group visits contributes to their group total.
5.  Time begins when the team breaks hands at the start station.
  -   i.e. everyone starts by holding hands in a circle
6.  Time ends when the group is all together and all stations have been
    visited.
  -   i.e. by all holding hands again
