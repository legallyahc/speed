MONTRÉAL MÉTRO SPEEDRUNNING

Rules

100% All Stations

This is the standard Montréal Métro speedrun category. The goal is to visit every station as fast as possible without splitting up a group (see Co-op). This can be done just for the Montréal Métro, or the Métro and Réseau Express Métropolitain (REM).

  • You must visit all station platforms.
  • If you are on a train, you do not need to step off at every station so long as your train stops at every station.
  • Time starts when the doors close on your first train, and end when doors open at your final station.
  • Communal public transportation only.
  • i.e. no cars, bikes, bixi, or taxis. Métro, REM, EXO, and buses only.
  • If exiting the station for a pause, time is paused when you exit the fare line and restarts when you re-cross the fare line.
  • This can be done in group so long as all members visit all stations together.
  • You do not need to ensure your fare is valid for your entire trip (though legally you should).

100% All Stations Co-op

This is a Métro speedrun with multiple teams. The goal is to split into separate teams that then cumulatively visit all stations.

  • All Non-coop rules except:
  • All runners must begin and end at the same station.
  • Runners do not need to visit all stations together.
  • Time begins when the runners part at the origin station and time ends when all runners are together at the end station.

Other cities!

There's a massive collection of other people who do this, too! While some of the most famous have a Guinness World Record, there are other 'home grown' collections like this. BART (San Francisco) collects records for their own system, DC previously had a similar blog about it, and the MBTA (Boston) is being revitalized with more complete rules after a recent extension!

This website is free! You can just take it! Hollar @me if you make one for your own city!
